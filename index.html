Rocket League Scoreboard

This webpage was made for RL casters to easily update their own scores.

How to use

  • Click top half of score to +1
  • Click bottom half of score to -1
  • Click on row to edit name, and also change the score colour
  • Press enter to add a new team/name
  • Leave the team/name blank and click off to remove it

This is designed to work as a browser source in streaming software

Useful CSS (transparent background)

body { background-color: transparent; margin: 0px auto; overflow: hidden; }

I hope you can find it useful!
